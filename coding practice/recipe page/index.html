Grandmas Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 meddile-size apple, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Per-heat over to 375 F (190C).
  2. In a bowl, mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
